## About Coinut Ramp

Coinut Ramp is the crypto-fiat infrastructure layer built by Coinut, a regulated digital asset trading and payments platform founded in 2013. We operate across **Singapore, Switzerland, Canada, and USA**, providing compliant payment rails to businesses worldwide.

Coinut Ramp lets you offer cryptocurrency services to your customers without building complex blockchain infrastructure or navigating regulatory requirements yourself.

## How to Use

Two integration paths — use either or both.

| Mode | Best For | How It Works |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **API** | Enterprise, platforms, marketplaces | Direct API calls, bulk operations, you manage customer aggregation and UX |
| **Dashboard** | Operations, support, quick lookups | Web UI for managing customers, virtual accounts, and transactions |

<Note>
  The Dashboard is **partially available** — customer management, virtual accounts, and trade/deposit tracking are already functional. Additional features (autoramp flows, address management, API key management) are under development. All capabilities are fully available through the API.
</Note>

## Architecture

Your application sends signed HTTPS requests to the Coinut Ramp API. We handle the complexity of banking integrations, blockchain transactions, compliance checks, and liquidity management. You receive webhook notifications for every state change.

## Licensing & Compliance

| Jurisdiction | License / Registration | Authority |
| --- | --- | --- |

We perform KYC/AML screening, transaction monitoring, and regulatory reporting so you don't have to.

## Key Features

- **HMAC-SHA256 Authentication** — Every request cryptographically signed
- **Real-time Webhooks** — Instant notifications for deposits, withdrawals, and state changes
- **Sandbox Environment** — Test end-to-end flows without real money
- **Multi-currency Support** — Current public examples focus on AUD, EUR, GBP, USD, and USDT. Confirm any additional corridors directly against your enabled account setup before launch.
- **Global Compliance** — Licensed and registered across the jurisdictions listed below, with automated KYC/AML controls
