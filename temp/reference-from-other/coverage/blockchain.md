> USDC and EURC are issued by Circle. EURe is issued by Monerium. USDT is issued by Tether. USDe is issued by Ethena. M is issued by M^0 and powers [MoonPay Issuance](https://docs.iron.xyz/issuance/create-your-stablecoin), the platform for launching branded stablecoins on Iron’s rails.

> Chains marked **On demand** can be enabled per partner. Contact your account manager to scope a new chain or stablecoin. New chains typically go live within two weeks.

### MoonPay-Issued Stablecoins

These stablecoins are issued by MoonPay through the [Issuance product](https://docs.iron.xyz/issuance/create-your-stablecoin). Approved partners can directly mint and redeem supply against fiat reserves via the Iron API.

| Stablecoin | Partner | Chain | Status |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **ctUSD** | Citrea | Citrea | Live |
| **XO** | Exodus | Solana | Live |

> Mint and redeem windows are Monday to Friday, 9:00 PM ET (previous day) to 5:00 PM ET. Requests submitted outside the window are processed at the start of the next window. See the [Issuance FAQ](https://docs.iron.xyz/issuance/create-your-stablecoin#faq) for full details.
