## Full On/Offramp Support

These currencies support both onramp (fiat to crypto) and offramp (crypto to fiat) for Business-to-Business (B2B), Business-to-Individual (B2C), and Individual-to-Individual (I2I) transactions.

| Currency | Payment Method | Average Delivery Speed |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **USD** | ACH, FedWire | Same Day (T+1 if outside cutoff time) |
| **EUR** | SEPA Instant (SEPA Credit if 100k+ EUR) | Real-time |
| **GBP** | Faster Payments | Real-time |
| **BRL (soon)** | PIX | Real-time |

## Virtual Account Availability

The following virtual accounts are available to Retail and Business customers by region.

| Region | USD Virtual Account | EUR Virtual Account | GBP Virtual Account |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| **USA** | Retail + Business | Retail + Business | Retail + Business |
| **Rest of World** | Retail + Business | Retail + Business | Retail + Business |
| **EU** | Business only | Retail + Business | Retail + Business |
| **UK** | Business only | Retail + Business | Retail + Business |
