## Overview

Payments enable partners to send fiat payouts (offramp) to their customers' bank
accounts. The Coinut partner platform currently supports **USD payouts**, where
you pay with USDT or USDC and the recipient receives USD in their bank account.

## Payment Flow

1. **Estimate** — Call `GET /payment/estimate` to preview the exchange rate,
   deducted amount, bank charges, and target bank account details before
   committing.

2. **Create** — Call `POST /payment/create` to initiate the payment. You specify
   the deduct currency (USDT / USDC), the fiat amount, the target bank account,
   and a reference. Optionally attach a supporting document.

3. **Track** — Poll `GET /payment/detail` or `GET /payment/list` to monitor the
   payment. When settled, a `PAYMENT_SETTLED` webhook is fired.

## Supported Currencies

| Direction | Currency |
|---|---|
| **Pay with** | `USDT`, `USDC` |
| **Receive** | `USD` |
